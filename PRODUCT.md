# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Everyone seeking motivation, regardless of their current circumstances. Users range from those needing a resilient push during a gloomy rainy day to those looking to amplify their energy on a bright sunny morning.

## Product Purpose
Dawncast provides a moment of reflection and a daily boost of motivation tailored to the user's local environment. It aims to let people "take a breath" and find perspective through quotes that resonate with the current weather and atmosphere.

## Brand Personality
- **Structured Serenity**: Clean, minimalist, and "enterprise-y" in its reliability and polish, but fundamentally calm and approachable.
- **Atmospheric**: Deeply connected to the user's physical world, shifting its mood with the sky.
- **Serene & Reliable**: Professional quality that feels like a trusted daily ritual.

## Anti-references
- **Over-stimulating SaaS**: Avoid cluttered dashboards, aggressive "gamification", or loud, high-contrast interfaces that break the moment of calm.
- **Generic "Inspiration" App**: Avoid cliché, overly-sentimental or "Pinterest-style" aesthetics that lack professional structure.

## Design Principles
- **Breath Before Action**: The interface should prioritize stillness and legibility, ensuring the user can digest the quote without distraction.
- **Environmental Resonance**: Visuals must adapt to the weather, not just for aesthetics, but to acknowledge and validate the user's current environment (e.g., resilience in rain, energy in sun).
- **Enterprise-Grade Minimalism**: Use precise spacing, high-quality typography, and a structured layout to provide a professional, reliable experience that doesn't feel like a toy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA Compliance**: High contrast for readability across all weather themes.
- **Reduced Motion**: Respect system preferences for animations, especially in the AR simulation and weather transitions.
- **Visual Diversity**: Ensure the "enterprise-y" look remains inclusive and warm across all atmospheric states.
